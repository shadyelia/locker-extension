Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Locker Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

Local Storage

The extension stores information such as your locked websites list, time limits, and lock preferences locally on your device using the Chrome storage API. This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted or shared.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

Usage of Permissions

Permissions requested by the extension (e.g., storage, tabs, and host access) are only used to enable the core locking functionality. These permissions are never used for tracking or analytics.

Changes

If we make any changes to this policy, we will update this file and the effective date.
